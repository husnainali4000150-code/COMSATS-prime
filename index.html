📋 NTS Test Pattern

Total: 90 MCQs | Marks: 100 | Time: 120 Minutes

1. Verbal Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:

  • Reading Comprehension (6-8 Questions)
  • Sentence Completion
  • Analogies & Word Relationships
  • Synonyms & Antonyms
  • Grammar & Error Detection
  • Paragraph Summary
  • Vocabulary Usage
2. Analytical Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:

  • Logic Puzzles & Arrangements
  • Seating Arrangements (Circular/Linear)
  • Scheduling Problems
  • Blood Relations
  • Direction Sense
  • Syllogisms & Venn Diagrams
  • Logical Deductions
  • Series Completion
3. Quantitative Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:

  • Arithmetic (Percentage, Ratio, Profit/Loss)
  • Algebra & Equations
  • Geometry & Mensuration
  • Data Interpretation (Graphs/Charts)
  • Time, Speed & Distance
  • Probability & Statistics
  • Number Systems
  • Simple & Compound Interest
4. Physics 10 MCQs

Topics Covered:

  • Mechanics & Motion
  • Electricity & Magnetism
  • Waves & Optics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Modern Physics
  • Nuclear Physics Basics
5. Computer / Chemistry 10 MCQs

Computer Topics:

  • Basic IT & Computer Fundamentals
  • MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Programming Basics
  • Database Concepts
  • Internet & Networking Basics

Chemistry Topics:

  • Organic Chemistry
  • Inorganic Chemistry
  • Physical Chemistry
  • Chemical Reactions & Bonding
6. Mathematics / Biology 10 MCQs

Mathematics Topics:

  • Calculus Basics
  • Trigonometry
  • Matrices & Determinants
  • Conic Sections

Biology Topics:

  • Cell Biology
  • Genetics & Evolution
  • Human Physiology
  • Ecology & Environment
📌 Total: 90 MCQs | Total Marks: 100 | Time Allowed: 120 Minutes

⭐ Important Topics for NTS

Focus on these high-weightage topics for better scores

📖 Verbal Reasoning - Key Topics
  • Most Repeated: Synonyms (5-6 Qs), Antonyms (4-5 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Reading Comprehension (6-8 Qs)
  • Important: Analogies, Sentence Completion
  • Tip: Learn 20 new words daily, practice comprehension daily
🧩 Analytical Reasoning - Key Topics
  • Most Repeated: Seating Arrangements (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Logic Puzzles (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Blood Relations, Direction Sense
  • Tip: Draw diagrams for every question
🔢 Quantitative Reasoning - Key Topics
  • Most Repeated: Percentages, Ratios (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Algebra & Equations (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Data Interpretation
  • Tip: Memorize formulas, practice shortcut methods
