📋 NTS Test Pattern

Total: 90 MCQs | Marks: 100 | Time: 120 Minutes

1. Verbal Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics: Reading Comprehension, Sentence Completion, Analogies, Synonyms & Antonyms, Grammar, Vocabulary

2. Analytical Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics: Logic Puzzles, Seating Arrangements, Blood Relations, Direction Sense, Syllogisms, Venn Diagrams

3. Quantitative Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics: Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Data Interpretation, Time-Speed-Distance, Probability

4. Physics 10 MCQs

Topics: Mechanics, Electricity, Waves, Thermodynamics, Modern Physics

5. Computer / Chemistry 10 MCQs

Computer: IT Basics, MS Office, Programming, DBMS

Chemistry: Organic, Inorganic, Physical Chemistry

6. Math / Biology 10 MCQs

Math: Calculus, Trigonometry, Matrices

Biology: Cell Biology, Genetics, Human Physiology

📌 Total: 90 MCQs | Marks: 100 | Time: 120 Minutes

📄 NTS Sample Paper (Solved)

Section 1: Verbal Reasoning (Sample)

Q1. Choose the synonym of "ABUNDANT":
A) Scarce
B) Plentiful ✓
C) Limited
D) Rare
✅ Correct Answer: B) Plentiful
Q2. Complete the sentence: "She was _____ tired that she fell asleep immediately."
A) very
B) so ✓
C) too
D) much
✅ Correct Answer: B) so

Section 2: Analytical Reasoning (Sample)

Q3. A is older than B. C is older than A. Who is the oldest?
A) A
B) B
C) C ✓
D) Cannot be determined
✅ Correct Answer: C) C

Section 3: Quantitative Reasoning (Sample)

Q4. What is 25% of 200?
A) 25
B) 50 ✓
C) 75
D) 100
✅ Correct Answer: B) 50
Q5. If x + 5 = 12, then x = ?
A) 5
B) 6
C) 7 ✓
D) 8
✅ Correct Answer: C) 7

📚 NTS Syllabus & Important Topics

📖 Verbal Reasoning - Important Topics
  • Most Repeated: Synonyms (5-6 Qs), Antonyms (4-5 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Reading Comprehension (6-8 Qs)
  • Important: Analogies, Sentence Completion, Grammar
  • Study Tip: Learn 20 new words daily
🧩 Analytical Reasoning - Important Topics
  • Most Repeated: Seating Arrangements (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Logic Puzzles (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Blood Relations, Direction Sense
  • Study Tip: Practice drawing diagrams
🔢 Quantitative Reasoning - Important Topics
  • Most Repeated: Percentages, Ratios (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Algebra & Equations (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Data Interpretation, Time-Speed-Distance
  • Study Tip: Memorize formulas & shortcuts
