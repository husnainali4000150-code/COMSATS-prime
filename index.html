📋 NTS Test Pattern

NTS (National Testing Service) Exam Structure - 90 MCQs, 100 Marks

1. Verbal Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Sentence Completion
  • Analogies
  • Antonyms & Synonyms
  • Grammar & Vocabulary
  • Paragraph Summary
  • Error Detection
  • Word Usage

2. Analytical Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:
  • Logic Puzzles
  • Seating Arrangements
  • Scheduling Problems
  • Blood Relations
  • Direction Sense
  • Syllogisms
  • Venn Diagrams
  • Logical Deductions

3. Quantitative Reasoning 20 MCQs

Topics Covered:
  • Arithmetic (%, Ratio, Profit/Loss)
  • Algebra & Equations
  • Geometry & Mensuration
  • Data Interpretation
  • Time, Speed, Distance
  • Probability & Statistics
  • Number Systems
  • Simple & Compound Interest

4. Physics 10 MCQs

Topics Covered:
  • Mechanics
  • Electricity & Magnetism
  • Waves & Optics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Modern Physics
  • Nuclear Physics

5. Computer / Chemistry 10 MCQs

Computer Topics:
  • Basic IT Concepts
  • MS Office
  • Programming Basics
  • Database Fundamentals
Chemistry Topics:
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Inorganic Chemistry
  • Physical Chemistry
  • Chemical Reactions

6. Mathematics / Biology 10 MCQs

Mathematics Topics:
  • Calculus
  • Trigonometry
  • Matrices & Determinants
  • Conic Sections
Biology Topics:
  • Cell Biology
  • Genetics
  • Human Physiology
  • Ecology
📌 Total: 90 MCQs | Total Marks: 100 | Time: 120 Minutes

⭐ Important Topics for NTS

📖 Verbal Reasoning - Key Topics

  • Most Repeated: Synonyms (5-6 Qs), Antonyms (4-5 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Reading Comprehension (6-8 Qs)
  • Important: Analogies, Sentence Completion
  • Tip: Focus on vocabulary building, learn 20 new words daily

🧩 Analytical Reasoning - Key Topics

  • Most Repeated: Seating Arrangements (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Logic Puzzles (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Blood Relations, Direction Sense
  • Tip: Practice drawing diagrams for each question

🔢 Quantitative Reasoning - Key Topics

  • Most Repeated: Percentages, Ratios (5-6 Qs)
  • High Weightage: Algebra & Equations (4-5 Qs)
  • Important: Data Interpretation, Time-Speed-Distance
  • Tip: Memorize formulas and practice shortcut methods

🔬 Subject-Specific - Key Topics

  • Physics: Mechanics, Electricity (most repeated)
  • Computer: MS Office, Basic IT terms
  • Chemistry: Organic chemistry reactions
  • Math: Calculus basics, Trigonometry formulas
  • Biology: Cell structure, Human body systems
  • Tip: Focus on your own subject (Computer or Chemistry, Math or Bio)
